Hello!

This is a basic communication assistance app for people who have difficulty speaking.

The conversation button leads to a list of conversational phrases; just select a phrase to say it.

You can also select the keyboard, type in a phrase, and select "speak" to generate a phrase in your own words.

Don't forget to turn up the volume on your device!
